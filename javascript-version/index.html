Social Network Diagram of CRA-Relevant Policies

The overall classification of this visualization is UNCLASSIFIED. The point of contact for this product is Deloitte Consulting LLP. This visualization accounts for 174 policies out of 935 policies identified as CRA relevant, policies with only one reference relationship were excluded. A legend to the right of the diagram helps identify the organizational level of the policy by the color of the dot, this legend also functions as a filter for organizational level of the policies. Drop down filters for policy number and issuing organization are at the top right of the diagram.
